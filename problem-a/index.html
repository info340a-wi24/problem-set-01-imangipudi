Shrek ✨

Shrek is a green ogre starring as the main character in the Shrek franchise movies as follows: Shrek, Shrek 2, Shrek the Third, Shrek Forever After, Scared Shrekless and Shrek The Halls. He valiantly rescues a princess stuck in a tower and guarded by a fire breathing dragon in order to fight for his swamp and makes friends along the way. The franchise follows his life, experiences, and lessons.
