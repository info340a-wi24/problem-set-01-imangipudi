Shrek

Shrek is a green ogre starring as the main character in the Shrek franchise movies as follows: Shrek, Shrek 2, Shrek the Third, Shrek Forever After, and Shrek The Halls.

  • Shrek broke into fairy godmothers factory and posed as the workers union in order to steal a potion for Fiona and drank it without knowing the consequences, he was willing to give up his life as an ogre and become a human if that was what made Fiona happy.
  • Shrek became friends with Puss and Boots even though he tried to kill Shrek
  • Shrek was very cool in the "Bad Reputation" fighting montage in Duloc
